The Best Movies According To Laxmi Priya

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

One Piece

This is my all-time favorite anime. It's one of the Big 3, and I absolutely love its adventurous plot, strong character development, and emotional depth.

Plot/Summary:

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a spirited young pirate who dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates. His journey to find the legendary treasure known as the One Piece is filled with epic battles, lifelong friendships, and powerful lessons.

Avengers: Endgame

This is my favorite Hollywood movie of all time. The emotional moments, epic action sequences, and the sense of finality in the storyline make it unforgettable.

Plot/Summary:

After Thanos wipes out half the universe, the remaining Avengers unite for one last mission. With courage, sacrifice, and teamwork, they strive to undo the past and bring hope to the future.

Hi Nanna

This is my recent favorite Telugu movie. Its emotional storytelling, heartfelt performances, and the bond between father and daughter deeply moved me.

Plot/Summary:

Hi Nanna tells the story of Viraj, a loving single father raising his daughter, Mahi. As hidden truths come to light and Yashna, Mahi’s mother, returns with no memory, the story unfolds into a moving tale of love, loss, and healing.
